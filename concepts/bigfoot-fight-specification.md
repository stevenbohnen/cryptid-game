What kind of actions could Bigfoot take?

* Attack with arms  
* Bite  
* Throw rocks  
* Become invisible  
* Shift dimensions (essentially, teleportation)  
* Mind control (minor telepathic abilities, such as calming, disorientation, and compulsion to leave the area. Possible also communication.)

I think, in general, Bigfoot should move like lore-accurate Frankenstein’s monster: incredibly quick and agile, but in an awkward, unnatural, askew way. Probably also shares similar appearance, but more ape-like.

Other behaviors/traits

* Wood knocking  
* Omnivorous  
* Takes gifts such as food and jewelry  
* Moaning howls, grunts, screams, whistles, potentially their own language  
* Generally solitary, possibly have groups  
* Possibly nocturnal  
* Marked, bent, or woven trees/plants, possibly to mark territory  
* Very good senses (strong hearing, smell, etc.)  
* STINKY\!

Appearance

* LARGE (I think it should be scaled up a bit: wide framed, probably at least 2x the player’s size)  
* Matted, emaciated-looking  
* Wispy hair

Arena

* Somewhat densely wooded area, trees everywhere to hide behind but not so much that it’s visually stuffy  
* Lots of toppled trees and scattered branches on the ground  
* Some sort of vine-covered grotto in the middle, essentially the exact center of Bigfoot’s movement area

What information could prove its existence?

* Footprints (duh)  
* Scream (I think a true guttural Bigfoot howl would be the best evidence, probably elicited from striking a weak point)  
* Fur  
* Pictures (I think Bigfoot would avoid the camera, probably go invisible or teleport away when a camera is pointed at it. Some distraction would be necessary.)
